🌟 Privacy First: Hydra Sprite is designed with your privacy as our top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

📱 About Hydra Sprite

Hydra Sprite is a water tracking application that helps you monitor your daily water intake with the help of a virtual sprite character. The app operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device.

🚫 What We Don't Collect

Hydra Sprite does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Any other personal data

💾 Local Data Storage

All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Daily water intake records
  • Sprite character state
  • App preferences and settings
  • Historical water tracking data

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

🌐 Internet Permission

While the app requests internet permission as required by the React Native framework, Hydra Sprite:

  • Does not make any network requests
  • Does not connect to any servers
  • Does not transmit any data
  • Functions completely offline

🔐 Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored externally:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security
  • No data breaches are possible from our side
  • You have complete control over your data

👶 Children's Privacy

Hydra Sprite is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

🔄 Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  1. Open the app and use the "Reset" function, or
  2. Uninstall the app from your device

This will permanently remove all water tracking data and preferences.

📋 Third-Party Services

Hydra Sprite does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

🌍 International Users

This privacy policy applies to all users worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns.
